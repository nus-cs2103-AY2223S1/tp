The EXPIRY_DATE is the date indicating when an item will expire.

**Note:**
- EXPIRY_DATE must be in the following format (dd-mm-yyyy)
- EXPIRY_DATE can only have years between 1900 and 2300 inclusive

**Valid Examples:**
- 01-01-2022
- 28-09-2022
- 11-11-2022

**Invalid Examples:**
- 1-1-2022
- 01-1-2022
- 1-01-2022
- 01/09/2022
- 40-40-2022
- 1-9-22
- 1-nov-2202
